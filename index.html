Idea - Instore Navigation

Needing to find things in store is another bummer! Its terrible to keep asking people for directions and and worse? Not finding someone to ask!! Even worse? Not buying the item which was available but could not be found in all these items.


We need an Interactive Instore Map, which can track our location within the store, as well as where are the different aisles and what are the items within the aisle!


On our left is an extremely simplified map, with the person being the circle, that can move around, as your mouse moves, within the map :)
